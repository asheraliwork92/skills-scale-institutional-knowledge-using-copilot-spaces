# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate Agile ceremonies, remove blockers, and help the team follow agreed processes. They coach the team on Agile practices and foster a culture of continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Identify and remove impediments blocking team progress
- Coach team members on Agile methodologies and best practices
- Track and communicate team velocity and sprint health
- Help resolve conflicts and maintain psychological safety
- Support PM and Developers in maintaining healthy team dynamics

### Goals
- Enable high-performing, self-organizing teams
- Maintain consistent sprint cadence and delivery rhythm
- Foster continuous improvement and learning culture
- Minimize process overhead while maximizing effectiveness

### Typical Communication
- Facilitation of Agile ceremonies
- Status reports on team health and velocity
- One-on-ones with team members
- Collaboration with PM on sprint planning and resource allocation

### Key Interactions
- Works closely with **Project Managers** to align schedules and dependencies
- Partners with **Developers** to remove technical blockers and optimize workflow
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on backlog refinement and prioritization

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure product usability and user satisfaction through research-driven design. They translate product requirements into intuitive, accessible user experiences that delight customers.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Design wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and metrics
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation and accessibility
- Document design patterns and create design systems
- Advocate for user needs and usability best practices

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support burden
- Increase user adoption and satisfaction
- Build consistency across product interfaces

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with stakeholders and engineers
- User research findings and insights
- Design specifications and implementation guides
- Feedback on feature acceptance and user testing

### Key Interactions
- Partners with **Product Managers** to understand customer needs and business goals
- Works with **Developers** to translate designs into code while maintaining fidelity
- Supports **QA/Testing** in validating user experience acceptance criteria
- Collaborates with **Customer Support Lead** on gathering user feedback and pain points

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers automate deployment, ensure system reliability, and maintain CI/CD pipelines. They bridge the gap between development and operations, enabling rapid, safe delivery to production.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain infrastructure as code
- Build and optimize CI/CD pipelines
- Manage production environments and monitoring systems
- Ensure system security, scalability, and reliability
- Coordinate deployments and rollbacks
- Optimize performance and reduce deployment friction

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable deployments with minimal manual effort
- Maintain high system uptime and performance
- Reduce mean time to recovery (MTTR) for incidents
- Provide observability and transparency into system health

### Typical Communication
- Deployment coordination and release notes
- Infrastructure and pipeline documentation
- Incident response and post-mortems
- Performance reports and optimization recommendations

### Key Interactions
- Partners with **Developers** on environment requirements, testing, and deployment best practices
- Coordinates with **QA/Testing** on test automation infrastructure and staging environments
- Supports **Project Managers** during release planning with deployment readiness assessments
- Works with **Release Leads** on deployment scheduling and risk mitigation

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts provide data-driven insights to support decision-making and product improvement. They define success metrics, gather data, and translate analysis into actionable recommendations.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key success metrics and KPIs
- Gather, analyze, and visualize product and business data
- Create dashboards and reports for stakeholder consumption
- Identify trends, patterns, and opportunities for improvement
- Conduct experiments and A/B tests to validate hypotheses
- Provide insights to inform product prioritization and strategy

### Goals
- Enable data-informed decision-making across the organization
- Measure and demonstrate product impact and ROI
- Identify optimization opportunities and growth drivers
- Reduce guesswork in product and business strategy

### Typical Communication
- Weekly and monthly metric reports
- Experiment results and recommendations
- Data visualizations and dashboards
- Insights presentations to leadership and product teams

### Key Interactions
- Collaborates closely with **Product Managers** to define metrics and interpret results
- Works with **Developers** to instrument code and ensure data quality
- Supports **Project Managers** in measuring project success against defined metrics
- Partners with **Customer Support Lead** to understand customer-facing metrics and feedback patterns

---

## Customer Support Lead

### Role Summary
Customer Support Leads act as the voice of the customer, managing incoming feedback and escalating issues. They ensure customer needs inform product decisions and that customer satisfaction drives continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Monitor support channels (email, chat, tickets, social media)
- Triage customer issues and escalate to appropriate teams
- Communicate product updates and changes to customers
- Gather customer feedback and pain points
- Report high-impact issues and trends to product and engineering teams
- Build customer-focused processes and documentation

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support volume through proactive solutions
- Ensure customer voice informs product development
- Identify and escalate critical business risks

### Typical Communication
- Weekly customer issue summaries and trends
- Escalation reports for high-impact problems
- Feature requests and customer feedback analysis
- Customer-facing documentation and FAQs

### Key Interactions
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on feature prioritization based on customer demand
- Partners with **Developers** to prioritize bug fixes and gather implementation feedback
- Works with **Project Managers** to ensure releases address known customer pain points
- Supports **Data Analysts** in identifying customer-relevant metrics and satisfaction drivers
